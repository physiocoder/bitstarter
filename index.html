What is MemorArt?
Is a new web application that revoluzionize art collections management. No need to download and install software, just register to our website and you can start organizing your art collections. From your pc or your tablet.
What can I do with MemorArt?
Store all images and information of every item in a collection. Organize different collections. You can shot artwork photos with your tablet or your webcam. Make Condition Report and sign them directly on tablet. Create temporary collections for art exhibitions and track all tasks and deadlines.
